The matching engine

Four tiers, strongest first. The engine proposes. Ops decides.

T1
Strict match
Reference + amount + date all match exactly.
No judgement needed. 1:1, clean.
Can auto-confirm
T2
Ref hit, amounts differ
Same reference, amount off by fees or FX.
Usually a wire-fee deduction.
Human decides
T3
Amount + date, no ref
Same amount, same day — no reference in narrative.
Statement without a description.
Human decides
T4
Amount ±1 day
Same amount, next-day booking.
Weekend / cut-off timing shift.
Human decides

The engine does the searching. Ops does the deciding.   Nothing auto-matches without a human confirm (except Tier 1 when enabled)

The right mental model: the engine removed the manual search. The judgement stays with ops.
