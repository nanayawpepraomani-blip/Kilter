The matching engine

Four tiers — the engine proposes, the human decides

Confidence pyramid
TierWhat matches
1 · StrictReference + amount + date
2 · Ref hitSame ref, amount off by fees/FX
3 · Amt + dateNo ref, same amount same day
4 · ±1 daySame amount, next-day booking
Tier 1 still needs one click — but it's one click, not a forensic exercise.
Nothing auto-matches without a human confirm
The engine isn't replacing the ops team. It's doing the searching — the part that burns hours — so the team can do the deciding — the part that actually needs judgement.
