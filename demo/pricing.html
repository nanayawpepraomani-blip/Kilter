Common questions

If your question isn't here, get in touch.

Is there a free trial?

No free trial — but the Pilot plan is designed to serve the same purpose with real skin in the game on both sides. A paid pilot means both parties are committed to a real outcome, not a slideware evaluation. At $7,500 / ₵84,000 it is well within a departmental discretionary budget.

What does the implementation fee cover?

Server deployment, schema setup, initial account registration, user onboarding session, and a go-live sign-off call. For Enterprise it also includes custom format mapping for any non-standard GL extract and Oracle Flexcube extract configuration. There are no surprise fees after this — support and updates are included in the annual license.

How does the Pilot convert to Standard?

At the end of the 3-month pilot term, we invoice the annual Standard license (minus the Pilot fee already paid — so you pay $28,000 − $7,500 = $20,500 to complete year one). No data migration needed — the same database carries forward.

Can the GHS price be locked?

Yes. Contracts can be denominated in GHS with a fixed exchange rate agreed at signing, locked for 12 months. At renewal the rate is re-negotiated based on the prevailing Bank of Ghana mid-rate. USD-denominated contracts are also available and are recommended for multi-year commitments.

Does our data leave our servers?

No. Kilter is entirely self-hosted — it runs on your own VM or Docker host and never phones home. No telemetry, no cloud sync, no vendor access to your data. Your SWIFT statements and GL data stay inside your network perimeter at all times.

What happens at renewal?

Renewal invoices are sent 45 days before the license anniversary. You can upgrade tiers at renewal at the then-current pricing. Downgrading from Enterprise to Standard requires a 90-day notice. There is no auto-renew without a signed order form.

How many users are included?

All plans are unlimited users within your organisation. There are no per-seat fees. Pricing is based on accounts (for Pilot / Standard) or flat (Enterprise). Add as many ops staff, auditors, and internal control users as you need.

What's included in software updates?

The annual license includes all point releases and minor version upgrades (e.g. 1.1, 1.2). Major version upgrades (2.0+) are offered at a discounted upgrade price for existing customers. Security patches are always included and will be flagged as urgent when applicable.
