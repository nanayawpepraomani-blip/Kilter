Welcome to Kilter

Four short steps to your first reconciliation. This card disappears once you've ingested a session.

  1. 1
    Register your first nostro account
    Pair a SWIFT account number with the corresponding core-banking GL. Pick a currency and a BIC.
    Open Cash accounts →
  2. 2
    Drop a SWIFT statement into intake
    Paste an MT940/950 .out or camt.053/054 .xml into messages/swift/, or upload manually on Manual upload.
  3. 3
    Drop the matching GL export
    Place the core-banking .xlsx for that same account into messages/flexcube/ (or upload it on Manual upload).
  4. 4
    Run the scanner
    Files pair, sessions get created, the matching engine proposes candidates. Trigger from Scan messages or with this button.
