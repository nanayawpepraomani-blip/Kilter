Seed proof — anchor the balance chain

Upload the proof xlsx for this account. The Day-0 balance chain starts at 0; the proof's net (credits − debits) becomes the anchor. Subsequent daily Flex deltas must open at this value to be accepted. Re-seeding is refused — clear the anchor first if the wrong proof was loaded.

Clear anchor — reset the balance chain

This resets the account to "not seeded" so a fresh proof can be loaded. Prior seed/delta sessions and their open items stay in place — only the anchor is wiped. The reason is recorded in the audit log.
