# Privacy Policy — Kilter

**Status:** DRAFT for lawyer review.
**Form:** Public-facing privacy policy for the Kilter marketing site
(`kilter.com` or equivalent) and customer onboarding wizard.

> Lawyer note: Kilter is sold to banks as on-premises / private-cloud
> software. The bank, not Kilter, is the Controller of bank end-customer
> data. This policy therefore covers (a) website visitors, (b) prospects
> and demo users, (c) Authorised Users at customer banks who interact
> with Kilter Support — NOT the bank's downstream customers.

**Last updated:** [DATE]
**Effective:** [DATE]

Kilter Limited ("**Kilter**", "**we**", "**us**") respects your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use
it, and the rights you have. It applies to:

- Visitors to our website at [kilter.com] and any related subdomains.
- Prospective customers who request a demo or pilot.
- Authorised Users at customer organisations who contact our support
  team or attend our training.

It does **not** apply to data that customer organisations process within
their own deployments of the Kilter Software. In those deployments, the
customer is the Controller of the data, and the customer's own privacy
policy governs.

## 1. Who is the Controller?

For the purposes of this Policy, the Controller is **Kilter Limited**, a
company incorporated in Ghana with registered office at [ADDRESS].

Contact for privacy queries: **privacy@kilter.com** (or
[CONTACT EMAIL]).

## 2. Personal Data We Collect

### 2.1 When you visit our website
- IP address, user-agent, referrer, pages viewed, and timestamps —
  collected in server access logs.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics SDKs that profile you across
  sites. We do not embed Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, or
  similar.
- Strictly necessary cookies only (session cookie if you log in; no
  advertising or tracking cookies).

### 2.2 When you contact us or request a demo
- Name, business email, employer name, job title, country, and any
  free-text message you send us.
- If you book a meeting, calendar metadata (date/time of meeting only).

### 2.3 When you join a pilot or become a customer
- The contact details of your Authorised Users (name, email, role) so we
  can communicate about support, releases, and renewals.
- Any support-ticket content you submit (which may incidentally include
  technical metadata about your environment — please do not include
  personal data of your end customers in support tickets).

### 2.4 What we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your bank's transaction data on our servers in the
  default deployment. The Kilter Software runs in your environment.
- We do not collect biometric, health, racial, religious, or other
  special-category data.
- We do not collect data about children. Kilter is a B2B product and is
  not directed at individuals under 18.

## 3. How We Use Personal Data

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Replying to your enquiry; setting up a demo or pilot | Performance of a contract / legitimate interest in responding to inbound interest |
| Delivering the Software, providing support, sending release notes | Performance of a contract |
| Sending occasional product updates to existing customers | Legitimate interest, with opt-out in every email |
| Securing our website and detecting abuse | Legitimate interest in security |
| Complying with legal obligations (tax, accounting, regulatory) | Legal obligation |

We do **not** sell personal data. We do not use it for automated
decision-making with legal or similarly significant effects.

## 4. Sharing

We share personal data only with:

- **Service providers** acting as processors on our behalf (e.g., email
  hosting, calendaring, accounting). These providers are contractually
  bound and may not use your data for their own purposes.
- **Professional advisors** (lawyers, auditors, accountants) under
  professional confidentiality.
- **Authorities**, if compelled by valid legal process. We will
  challenge overly broad requests and will give you notice unless
  prohibited by law.
- **A successor** in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of
  substantially all of our assets, subject to equivalent privacy
  commitments.

We do not share or sell personal data for advertising.

## 5. International Transfers

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or
another jurisdiction with cross-border transfer rules, your personal
data may be transferred to Ghana (where we are headquartered) and to
service providers located in [REGIONS]. Where required, we rely on
Standard Contractual Clauses or other lawful transfer mechanisms.

## 6. Retention

| Category | Retention |
|---|---|
| Website server logs | 90 days |
| Demo / pilot enquiries that did not convert | 24 months from last contact |
| Customer support tickets | Term of the relationship + 36 months |
| Tax, accounting, and regulatory records | As required by law (typically 6 years in Ghana) |

## 7. Your Rights

Subject to Applicable Privacy Law, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Request deletion (subject to retention obligations).
- Object to or restrict certain processing.
- Receive your data in a portable format.
- Withdraw consent where we rely on consent.
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in Ghana, the **Data
  Protection Commission** ([dataprotection.org.gh]).

To exercise any of these rights, contact **privacy@kilter.com**. We will
respond within thirty (30) days, or sooner where required by law.

## 8. Security

We protect personal data with appropriate technical and organisational
measures, including encryption, access controls, MFA on all internal
systems, and regular review. No system is perfectly secure; if you
believe an account or data has been compromised, contact
**security@kilter.com** immediately.

## 9. Cookies

Our website uses only **strictly necessary** cookies — a session cookie
to keep you logged in if you sign in to a customer dashboard, and a
preference cookie to remember whether you accepted this notice. We do
not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Kilter is a B2B product. We do not knowingly collect personal data from
anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact
**privacy@kilter.com** and we will delete it.

## 11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be
notified by email to active customers and posted on our website at least
thirty (30) days before taking effect.
