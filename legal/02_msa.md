This Master Services Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is entered into as of
[EFFECTIVE DATE] (the "**Effective Date**") between:

- **Kilter Limited**, a company incorporated in Ghana with registered
  office at [ADDRESS] ("**Kilter**"); and
- **[CUSTOMER LEGAL NAME]**, [ENTITY TYPE] of [JURISDICTION] with

Each a "**Party**" and together the "**Parties**."

## 1. Definitions

- **"Software"** — the Kilter reconciliation platform, including all
  parsers, matching engines, web UI, APIs, scheduled jobs, and
  documentation, in object code form, as updated from time to time.
- **"Order Form"** — a document signed by both Parties referencing this
  Agreement and specifying the Software modules licensed, fees,
  deployment model, and term.
- **"Customer Data"** — all data uploaded by Customer to the Software or
  ingested by the Software on Customer's behalf, including financial
  messaging files (SWIFT MT940/MT950, ISO 20022 camt.053/054), general
  ledger extracts, and reconciliation outputs.
- **"Authorised Users"** — Customer's employees and contractors authorised
  by Customer to use the Software, up to the user count stated on the
  applicable Order Form.
- **"Documentation"** — Kilter's then-current user manuals and operator
  guides for the Software.
- **"Intellectual Property Rights"** — copyrights, patents, trademarks,
  trade secrets, database rights, and all other intellectual property
  rights worldwide.

## 2. Licence Grant

2.1 **Grant.** Subject to Customer's payment of the Fees and compliance
with this Agreement, Kilter grants Customer a non-exclusive,
non-transferable, non-sublicensable licence during the Term to install and
operate the Software solely for Customer's internal business operations,
limited to the user count and modules stated on the applicable Order Form.

2.2 **Deployment.** The Software is licensed for **on-premises or
private-cloud deployment** under Customer's control. Kilter does not host
Customer Data unless the Order Form expressly elects a managed-hosting
addendum.

2.3 **Restrictions.** Customer shall not, and shall not permit any third
party to: (a) reverse-engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Software,
except to the extent expressly permitted by mandatory law; (b) sublicense,
rent, lease, or resell the Software; (c) remove or alter any proprietary
notices; (d) use the Software to provide a service bureau, hosted, or
multi-tenant offering to third parties; (e) use the Software in violation
of any law; or (f) exceed the licensed user count or module scope.

## 3. Fees and Payment

3.1 **Fees.** Customer shall pay the fees set forth on the Order Form
("**Fees**") in the currency stated. Fees are exclusive of VAT,
withholding tax, and other taxes, which Customer shall pay in addition to
the Fees, except for taxes on Kilter's net income.

3.2 **Invoicing.** Kilter shall invoice annually in advance unless
otherwise stated on the Order Form. Invoices are due net thirty (30) days
from invoice date. Overdue amounts accrue interest at 1.5% per month or
the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.

3.3 **Suspension.** Kilter may suspend support and updates (but not
operation of an already-deployed instance) if Customer is more than
thirty (30) days overdue and fails to cure within ten (10) business days
of written notice.

3.4 **Renewal.** The Order Form auto-renews for successive one-year terms
unless either Party gives ninety (90) days' written notice of
non-renewal. Renewal Fees may increase by up to the lesser of (a) Ghana
CPI for the prior year plus 3%, or (b) 8%.

## 4. Term and Termination

4.1 **Term.** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and
continues for so long as any Order Form remains in effect.

4.2 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement
or any Order Form immediately on written notice if the other Party (a)
materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within
thirty (30) days of written notice; or (b) becomes insolvent, files for
bankruptcy, or has a receiver appointed.

4.3 **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (a) all licences granted
under affected Order Forms terminate; (b) Customer shall cease using the
Software and, at Kilter's request, certify destruction within thirty (30)
days; (c) Customer shall pay all Fees accrued through the termination
date; (d) Kilter shall provide a final export of Customer Data in CSV or
SQLite format within thirty (30) days; and (e) Sections 5, 6, 7, 8, 9,
10, 11 and 13 survive.

## 5. Intellectual Property

5.1 **Kilter IP.** Kilter and its licensors retain all right, title, and
interest in and to the Software, Documentation, and all derivative works
thereof. No rights are granted other than those expressly stated.

5.2 **Customer Data.** Customer retains all right, title, and interest in
and to Customer Data. Customer grants Kilter a limited licence to host,
copy, transmit, display, and process Customer Data solely as necessary to
provide the Software and services under this Agreement.

5.3 **Feedback.** If Customer provides feedback, suggestions, or feature
requests, Kilter may freely use such feedback without obligation to
Customer.

5.4 **Anonymised Data.** Kilter may collect aggregated, anonymised
operational metrics (e.g., parser performance, matching success rates,
file format distribution) for product improvement, provided no Customer
Data, counterparty identity, transaction amount, or personal data is
identifiable from such metrics.

## 6. Warranties

6.1 **Mutual.** Each Party represents that it has the authority to enter
this Agreement and that doing so does not violate any other agreement.

6.2 **Software Warranty.** Kilter warrants that during the Term the
Software will materially conform to the Documentation. Customer's sole
remedy for breach of this warranty is for Kilter to use commercially
reasonable efforts to correct the non-conformity, or, if Kilter cannot do
so within ninety (90) days, to refund the prepaid Fees for the
non-conforming portion of the Software.

6.3 **Disclaimer.** EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY STATED, THE SOFTWARE IS PROVIDED
"AS IS" WITHOUT WARRANTY OF ANY KIND, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING
WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE,
NON-INFRINGEMENT, OR THAT OPERATION WILL BE ERROR-FREE OR UNINTERRUPTED.
KILTER DOES NOT WARRANT THAT THE SOFTWARE WILL DETECT EVERY
RECONCILIATION BREAK OR FRAUDULENT TRANSACTION; CUSTOMER REMAINS
RESPONSIBLE FOR ITS OWN FINANCIAL CONTROLS AND REGULATORY COMPLIANCE.

## 7. Confidentiality

7.1 Each Party (the "**Receiving Party**") shall protect the
**Confidential Information** of the other (the "**Disclosing Party**")
with at least the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential
information, and not less than reasonable care. Confidential Information
includes the Software, Documentation, pricing, security architecture, and
any non-public business or technical information.

7.2 The Receiving Party shall not disclose Confidential Information to
any third party except its employees, contractors, and advisors with a
need to know who are bound by confidentiality obligations no less
protective than those in this Section.

7.3 Confidentiality obligations survive termination for five (5) years,
except for trade secrets, which shall be protected for so long as they
remain trade secrets under applicable law.

## 8. Limitation of Liability

8.1 **Liability Cap.** EXCEPT FOR THE EXCLUSIONS BELOW, EACH PARTY'S
AGGREGATE LIABILITY ARISING FROM OR RELATING TO THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT
EXCEED THE FEES PAID OR PAYABLE BY CUSTOMER UNDER THE APPLICABLE ORDER
FORM IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING
RISE TO THE CLAIM.

8.2 **Consequential Damages.** NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR
INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES,
INCLUDING LOST PROFITS, LOST REVENUE, OR LOSS OF DATA, EVEN IF ADVISED OF
THE POSSIBILITY.

8.3 **Exclusions from Cap.** The cap and exclusions above do not apply
to: (a) Customer's payment obligations; (b) either Party's breach of
Section 7 (Confidentiality); (c) Kilter's indemnification obligations
under Section 9; (d) Customer's breach of Section 2.3 (Restrictions); or
(e) liability for fraud, gross negligence, or wilful misconduct.

## 9. Indemnification

9.1 **By Kilter.** Kilter shall defend Customer against any third-party
claim that the Software, as provided by Kilter and used by Customer in
accordance with this Agreement, infringes a Ghanaian patent, copyright, or
registered trademark; and shall pay damages finally awarded against
Customer or amounts agreed in settlement. Kilter has no liability for
claims arising from (a) Customer Data, (b) Customer's combination of the
Software with non-Kilter products, or (c) Customer's modification of the
Software.

9.2 **By Customer.** Customer shall defend Kilter against any third-party
claim arising from Customer Data or Customer's misuse of the Software in
breach of this Agreement, and shall pay damages finally awarded against
Kilter or amounts agreed in settlement.

9.3 **Procedure.** The indemnified Party shall promptly notify the
indemnifying Party of the claim, allow the indemnifying Party sole
control of defence and settlement, and provide reasonable cooperation. No
settlement requiring an admission of liability or material non-monetary
obligation may be entered without the indemnified Party's consent.

## 10. Data Protection

The processing of personal data is governed by the **Data Processing
Addendum** at `legal/04_dpa.md`, which is incorporated by reference where
Customer Data includes personal data within the meaning of Ghana's Data
Protection Act 2012 (Act 843) or other applicable privacy law.

## 11. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

11.1 **Governing Law.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of the
Republic of Ghana, without regard to conflict of laws principles.

11.2 **Dispute Resolution.** The Parties shall first attempt to resolve
any dispute through good-faith negotiation between senior executives. If
unresolved within thirty (30) days, the dispute shall be referred to
arbitration in Accra, Ghana, under the rules of the Ghana Arbitration
Centre, before a single arbitrator. The language of arbitration shall be
English. Judgment on the award may be entered in any court of competent
jurisdiction.

> Lawyer note: For pilots with non-African counterparties, consider London
> (LCIA) or Singapore (SIAC) as alternative seats — banks often prefer
> them. Trade-off: more expensive but more enforceable internationally.

## 12. Force Majeure

Neither Party is liable for failure to perform (other than payment
obligations) due to causes beyond its reasonable control, including acts
of God, war, terrorism, civil unrest, government action, internet or
telecommunications failure, or pandemic.

## 13. General

13.1 **Notices.** All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the
addresses on the Order Form by registered mail, courier, or email with
confirmation of receipt.

13.2 **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the
other's written consent, except that either Party may assign to a
successor in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or
substantially all assets, on written notice.

13.3 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with any Order Forms
and the DPA, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and
supersedes all prior agreements regarding its subject matter. Pre-printed
terms on Customer purchase orders are rejected.

13.4 **Amendments.** No amendment is effective unless in writing and
signed by both Parties.

13.5 **Severability.** If any provision is held unenforceable, the
remaining provisions remain in full force, and the Parties shall
substitute an enforceable provision that most closely reflects the
original intent.

13.6 **No Waiver.** Failure to enforce any provision is not a waiver of
that or any other provision.

13.7 **Independent Contractors.** The Parties are independent
contractors. Nothing creates a partnership, joint venture, or agency
relationship.

13.8 **Counterparts.** This Agreement may be signed in counterparts and
electronically (including via DocuSign or equivalent), each of which
constitutes an original.
